Belarusian citizens can enter Albania without a visa for a duration of 30 days.

### Visa-Free Access

Turkey is granted visa-free access to Albania.

## Countries That Require a Visa

Only one nationality is required to obtain a visa to enter Albania. Citizens of Syria must secure a visa prior to their arrival in the country.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

Travelers planning to visit Albania should be aware of the entry requirements based on their nationality. It is crucial to have a valid passport that meets the minimum validity requirements, which is typically six months beyond the planned date of departure from Albania. 

For those applying for an e-visa, ensure that all necessary documents are prepared and submitted online well in advance of travel. It is advisable to check for any updates or changes in visa regulations prior to departure, as policies may evolve.

Travelers are encouraged to familiarize themselves with local customs and regulations to ensure a smooth entry into Albania. Having travel insurance and sufficient funds for the duration of the stay can also facilitate a hassle-free experience.
