## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

Travelers planning to visit Lesotho should ensure they meet the entry requirements based on their nationality. It is advisable to check the validity of travel documents, as passports should typically be valid for at least six months beyond the intended date of entry. Additionally, travelers may need to provide proof of onward travel and sufficient funds for their stay.

For those eligible for an e-visa, applying in advance can streamline the entry process. It is also recommended to familiarize oneself with local customs and regulations to ensure a smooth visit.

In summary, Lesotho's visa policy accommodates a wide range of nationalities, offering various options for entry. Understanding the specific requirements based on nationality will help ensure a successful travel experience to this unique destination.
