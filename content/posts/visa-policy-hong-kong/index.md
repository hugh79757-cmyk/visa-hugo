With a total of 198 nationalities recognized, the visa requirements can be categorized into several groups. Some travelers may enter without a visa, while others may need to apply for an e-visa or a traditional visa before their arrival. Understanding these requirements is essential for a smooth entry into Hong Kong.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When planning a trip to Hong Kong, it is important to be aware of the entry requirements based on your nationality. Ensure that your passport is valid for at least six months beyond your intended stay. Travelers should also have a return ticket and sufficient funds for their duration in Hong Kong. It is advisable to check for any updates or changes in visa policies before traveling, as regulations can change. 

![Photo](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-hong-kong/body_5.jpg)
