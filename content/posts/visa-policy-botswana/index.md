## Countries That Require a Visa

There is one nationality that requires a visa to enter Botswana. Citizens of Kosovo must obtain a visa prior to their arrival in the country. This requirement underscores the importance of checking visa regulations based on nationality before planning a trip.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When planning a trip to Botswana, it is essential to be aware of the entry requirements. All travelers, regardless of nationality, must possess a valid passport with at least six months of validity remaining from the date of entry. Additionally, travelers should have proof of onward travel, which may be requested by immigration officials upon arrival.

It is advisable for travelers to familiarize themselves with the local laws and customs of Botswana to ensure a smooth visit. Understanding the visa policy and entry requirements will help prevent any complications during the travel process.
