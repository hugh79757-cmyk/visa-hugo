## Countries That Require a Visa

Travelers from countries not listed under the visa-free or e-visa categories will need to obtain a traditional visa before entering Antigua and Barbuda. This requirement applies to various nationalities, and it is essential for travelers to check their specific visa requirements based on their nationality.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When traveling to Antigua and Barbuda, it is crucial to ensure that you meet all entry requirements. Aside from possessing a valid visa or e-visa, travelers should also have a passport that is valid for at least six months beyond their planned departure date from Antigua and Barbuda. Additionally, travelers may be asked to provide proof of onward travel and sufficient funds for their stay.

It is advisable for travelers to familiarize themselves with the local customs and regulations to ensure a smooth entry process. Checking for any updates on entry requirements before traveling is also recommended, as policies may change.
