## Djibouti Visa Policy Overview

Djibouti, located in the Horn of Africa, has a visa policy that accommodates a wide range of nationalities. The country offers various entry options depending on the passport held by the traveler. With a total of 198 nationalities recognized, Djibouti provides a mix of visa-free access, visa-on-arrival options, and e-visa facilities. This guide outlines the specific requirements for each nationality, ensuring travelers are well-informed before their journey.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

![visa-policy-djibouti](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-djibouti/body_5.jpg)


When traveling to Djibouti, it is essential to ensure that your passport is valid for at least six months beyond your planned date of entry. Additionally, travelers should have a return ticket and proof of accommodation during their stay. It is advisable to check for any health requirements, such as vaccinations, particularly for travelers coming from regions with health risks.

Travelers should also be aware of the local customs and regulations to ensure a smooth entry process. Having all necessary documentation readily available can facilitate a hassle-free arrival in Djibouti.
