## Egypt Visa Policy Overview

Egypt, known for its long history and cultural heritage, has a structured visa policy that governs the entry of foreign nationals. With a total of 198 nationalities, Egypt offers various visa options tailored to different countries. Understanding these requirements is essential for travelers planning to visit Egypt, whether for tourism, business, or other purposes. The visa policy categorizes countries into several groups based on their visa requirements, including those that can enter visa-free, those eligible for an e-visa, and those that need to obtain a visa prior to arrival.

## e-Visa and ETA Options

![visa-policy-egypt](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-egypt/body_3.jpg)


Egypt offers an e-visa option for citizens of five specific countries. Travelers from these nations can apply for an electronic visa prior to their arrival in Egypt. The countries eligible for the e-visa are:

- Bosnia and Herzegovina
- India
- Iraq
- Kosovo
- Moldova

This digital application process simplifies the entry procedure for travelers, allowing them to obtain their visa online without the need to visit an embassy or consulate.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

![visa-policy-egypt](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-egypt/body_5.jpg)


Travelers planning to enter Egypt should be aware of the entry requirements beyond visa regulations. It is advisable to have a valid passport with at least six months of validity remaining from the date of entry. Additionally, travelers should ensure they have proof of onward travel and sufficient funds for their stay.

Health and safety regulations may also be in place, so it is recommended to check for any vaccination requirements or health advisories before traveling. Familiarizing oneself with local customs and laws can enhance the travel experience and ensure compliance with Egyptian regulations.

In summary, understanding the visa policy of Egypt is crucial for a smooth travel experience. Whether you are eligible for visa-free entry, a visa on arrival, or need to apply for a visa in advance, being informed will help you plan your visit effectively.
