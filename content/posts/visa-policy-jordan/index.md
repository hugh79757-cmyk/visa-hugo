### Visa-Free Access for 30 Days

Travelers from Egypt and Palestine can enter Jordan without a visa for a duration of up to 30 days.

Citizens from the following countries can enjoy visa-free access for up to 90 days:

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When planning a trip to Jordan, it is important to be aware of the entry requirements. Travelers should ensure that their passports are valid for at least six months beyond their planned date of entry. It is also advisable to have a return ticket and proof of accommodation during the stay in Jordan.

![Photo](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-jordan/body_4.jpg)

For those eligible for visa-on-arrival, it is recommended to carry sufficient funds to cover expenses during the visit. Additionally, travelers should be prepared to provide any necessary documentation that may be requested by immigration authorities upon arrival.

In summary, understanding the visa policy of Jordan is crucial for a smooth entry into the country. By knowing whether you require a visa, can enter visa-free, or are eligible for a visa-on-arrival, you can better prepare for your journey.
