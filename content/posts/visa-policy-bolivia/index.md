### Visa-Free Access for 30 Days

Honduras is the only country whose citizens can enter Bolivia without a visa for up to 30 days.

## e-Visa and ETA Options

Bolivia offers an e-visa option for travelers from Sudan. This electronic visa allows for a more streamlined application process, enabling travelers to apply online before their journey. 

## Countries That Require a Visa

All other nationalities not mentioned in the previous sections will need to apply for a visa prior to their arrival in Bolivia. This includes travelers from countries not eligible for visa-free entry or visa on arrival. It is essential for these travelers to check the specific visa application requirements and procedures applicable to their nationality.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When planning a trip to Bolivia, it is important to consider several practical tips. Travelers should ensure that their passport is valid for at least six months beyond their planned date of entry. Additionally, it is advisable to have proof of onward travel and sufficient funds for the duration of the stay. 

Travelers should also be aware of any health requirements, such as vaccinations, that may be necessary for entry into Bolivia. It is recommended to stay informed about the latest travel advisories and entry regulations, as these can change frequently. 

In summary, understanding Bolivia's visa policy is crucial for a successful entry into the country. By knowing whether a visa is required, and what type of visa applies, travelers can better prepare for their journey.
