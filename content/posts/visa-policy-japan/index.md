With a total of 198 nationalities recognized, the visa requirements vary significantly based on the applicant's nationality. Some travelers can enter Japan without a visa, while others must obtain one prior to their arrival. This guide provides an overview of the different visa categories, including visa-free access, e-visa options, and the nationalities that require a visa to enter Japan.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

Travelers planning to visit Japan should be aware of the general entry requirements. Regardless of visa status, all visitors must possess a valid passport with at least six months of validity remaining from the date of entry. Additionally, travelers may be required to provide proof of onward travel and sufficient funds to support their stay in Japan.
