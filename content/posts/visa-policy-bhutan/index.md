## Countries That Require a Visa

While many nationalities can enter Bhutan through the e-visa system, there are no specific countries listed that require a traditional visa application process outside of the e-visa option. Therefore, it is essential for travelers from the 195 nationalities mentioned above to apply for an e-visa before their travel to Bhutan.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When planning a trip to Bhutan, travelers should be aware of the entry requirements associated with their visa status. For those who are eligible for the e-visa, it is advisable to complete the application well in advance of travel to ensure a smooth entry process. The e-visa is typically linked to the traveler's passport, and it is important to carry a printed copy of the e-visa upon arrival.

Travelers should also ensure that their passport is valid for at least six months beyond their intended stay in Bhutan. It is recommended to have a return ticket and proof of accommodation during the visit, as these may be requested by immigration officials upon arrival.

In summary, Bhutan's visa policy is designed to facilitate the entry of foreign nationals while ensuring that the process remains efficient and organized. By understanding the visa requirements and preparing accordingly, travelers can enjoy their time in Bhutan with minimal hassle.
