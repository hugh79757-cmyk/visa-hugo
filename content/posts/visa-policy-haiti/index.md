## e-Visa and ETA Options

Currently, Haiti does not offer an e-Visa or Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) system for travelers. All visitors must adhere to the visa requirements based on their nationality, either entering visa-free or obtaining a visa prior to their arrival.

## Countries That Require a Visa

While the majority of nationalities can enter Haiti without a visa, there are five countries whose citizens must obtain a visa before traveling to Haiti. These countries are:

Colombia, Cuba, Dominican Republic, Palestine, Panama.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

Travelers planning to visit Haiti should be aware of the general entry requirements. A valid passport is essential, and it should be valid for at least six months beyond the date of entry. Additionally, travelers may be asked to provide proof of onward travel and sufficient funds for their stay.

It is advisable for visitors to check for any updates or changes in the visa policy prior to their trip, as regulations can change. Understanding the local customs and regulations can also enhance the travel experience in Haiti.
