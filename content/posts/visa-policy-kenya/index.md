### Visa-Free Access

The following countries can enter Kenya without a visa:

- **Burundi**
- **DR Congo**
- **Rwanda**
- **Somalia**
- **South Sudan**
- **Tanzania**
- **Uganda**

## Countries That Require a Visa

Travelers from the remaining nationalities not mentioned in the visa-free or ETA categories will need to obtain a visa prior to entering Kenya. This includes nationals from countries not listed in the e-Visa or ETA options. It is advisable for these travelers to check with the nearest Kenyan embassy or consulate for specific visa application procedures and requirements.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When planning a trip to Kenya, it is essential to be aware of the entry requirements. Apart from a valid visa or ETA, travelers should ensure their passport is valid for at least six months beyond their planned departure date from Kenya. It is also recommended to have a return ticket and proof of accommodation during the stay.

![Photo](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-kenya/body_5.jpg)
*Photo by [Gregory Odhiambo](https://www.pexels.com/@gregory-odhiambo-184980250) on [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com)*


Travelers should familiarize themselves with the customs regulations and health requirements, including vaccinations that may be necessary for entry. It is advisable to stay updated on any travel advisories or changes in visa policies that may affect travel plans.

 understanding the visa requirements for entering Kenya is crucial for a smooth travel experience. By knowing whether a visa is needed and the options available, travelers can better prepare for their visit to this diverse and scenic destination.
