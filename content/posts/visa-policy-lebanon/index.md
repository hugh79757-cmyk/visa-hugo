### Visa-Free Access for 180 Days

Citizens from the following countries can enter Lebanon without a visa and stay for up to 180 days:

- Bahrain
- Jordan
- Kuwait
- Oman
- Qatar
- Saudi Arabia

## e-Visa and ETA Options

Currently, Lebanon does not offer an e-Visa or Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) option for travelers. All visa applications must be processed through the appropriate channels, either by obtaining a visa in advance or securing a visa on arrival upon entering the country.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When planning a trip to Lebanon, travelers should be aware of the entry requirements. A valid passport is essential, and it should remain valid for at least six months beyond the date of entry. Travelers should also have proof of onward travel and sufficient funds for their stay.
