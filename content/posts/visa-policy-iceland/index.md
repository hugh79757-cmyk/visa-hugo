## e-Visa and ETA Options

Currently, Iceland does not offer an e-Visa or Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) system for travelers. All individuals from countries that require a visa must apply for a traditional visa through the appropriate channels before traveling to Iceland.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

When planning a trip to Iceland, it is essential to be aware of the entry requirements. Travelers must ensure that their passport is valid for at least three months beyond their planned departure date from the Schengen Area. Additionally, travelers may be required to show proof of sufficient funds for their stay and a return ticket.
