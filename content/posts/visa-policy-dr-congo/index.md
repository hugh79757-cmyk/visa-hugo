## DR Congo Visa Policy Overview

The Democratic Republic of the Congo (DR Congo) has specific visa policies that dictate who can enter the country and under what conditions. With a total of 198 nationalities, the visa requirements vary significantly based on the traveler’s country of origin. Understanding these requirements is essential for anyone planning to visit DR Congo, whether for tourism, business, or other purposes.

## Visa-Free and Visa-on-Arrival Access

![visa-policy-dr-congo](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-dr-congo/body_2.jpg)


Certain nationalities enjoy the privilege of entering DR Congo without a visa. There are two categories of visa-free access: those who can enter visa-free and those who can obtain a visa on arrival.

### Visa-Free Access

Travelers from the following countries can enter DR Congo without a visa:

- Burundi
- Rwanda

### Visa-Free for 90 Days

Citizens of the following countries can stay in DR Congo for up to 90 days without a visa:

- Congo
- Zimbabwe

### Visa on Arrival

Travelers from the following countries can obtain a visa upon arrival in DR Congo:

- Kenya
- Mauritius
- Tanzania

## e-Visa and ETA Options

![visa-policy-dr-congo](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-dr-congo/body_3.jpg)


For the majority of nationalities, DR Congo offers an e-visa option. This electronic visa system simplifies the application process, allowing travelers to apply online before their trip. The e-visa is available to 191 nationalities, making it a convenient choice for many.

## Entry Requirements and Practical Tips

![visa-policy-dr-congo](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/visa-policy-dr-congo/body_5.jpg)


Travelers planning to visit DR Congo should be aware of the entry requirements and practical considerations. It is advisable to ensure that your passport is valid for at least six months beyond your intended stay. Additionally, travelers should have a return ticket and proof of accommodation during their stay.

For those applying for an e-visa, it is essential to complete the application accurately and provide all required documentation to avoid delays. It is also recommended to check for any health advisories or vaccination requirements prior to travel, as certain vaccinations may be necessary for entry into DR Congo.

Understanding these visa policies and requirements will help ensure a smooth entry into DR Congo for all travelers.
